Time Complexity Comparison


Linear Search:

Best Case: O(1) - The target product is the first element.
Average Case: O(n) - The target product is somewhere in the middle or at the end.
Worst Case: O(n) - The target product is the last element or not present.


Binary Search:

Best Case: O(1) - The target product is the middle element.
Average Case: O(log n) - The target product is found after a few divisions of the array.
Worst Case: O(log n) - The target product is the last element to be found or not present.
